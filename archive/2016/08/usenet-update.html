  • I have given thunderbird the middle finger for download usenet articles1
  • I found nget, its an old program, but still works :)
nget -dfim --host $url \n
 --no-decode -m yes \n
 -P '$folder' \n
 -g $group \n
 -r ""

$url is the nttp server
$folder is were you want the articles saved
$group is the group you want to download
